The Best Movies According to Tanishq

My all time fav movies (Top 3)

1. Fast and Furious 8

The movie delivers non-stop action and thrilling car chases, but falls short in terms of plot depth and character development.Fans of the franchise will enjoy the adrenaline-fueled ride, but others may find it lacking substance.

2. Terminator 2

The movie is an iconic sci-fi film that combines groundbreaking visual effects with a gripping story, showcasing Arnold Schwarzenegger at his best as the heroic T-800. It is a thrilling and emotional ride that solidifies its place as one of the greatest action movies of all time.

3. Transporter 2

The movie is a high-octane action film that delivers intense car chases and well-choreographed fight sequences, but lacks the depth and originality of its predecessor. While it offers entertaining thrills, it falls short in terms of plot and character development.
